dental implant process

Guiding You to Optimal Oral Health

01

initial examination

Your journey begins with a comprehensive dental assessment. We ensure a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs and comfort.

02

tooth extraction

If necessary, we gently remove the damaged tooth to prepare the site for implant placement, prioritizing patient care at every step.

03

bone grafting

In cases of low bone density, a bone graft is performed to support the implant. This ensures long-term stability and a strong foundation.

04

crown placement

After healing, a custom-made crown is securely attached to the implant—restoring the natural look, feel, and function of your smile.
